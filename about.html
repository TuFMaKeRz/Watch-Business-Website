Our Story

Passion for precision since 2005

Our Heritage

Founded in 2005 by master watchmaker James Horologist, TimeCrafters began as a small repair shop with a big vision. What started as a one-man operation has grown into New York's premier destination for luxury watch sales and service.

Our commitment to excellence has earned us recognition from some of the most prestigious watch brands in the world. We're proud to be an authorized dealer and service center for Rolex, Omega, Patek Philippe, and other leading manufacturers.

At TimeCrafters, we believe a fine watch is more than just a timepiece - it's a legacy. Whether you're purchasing your first luxury watch or maintaining a family heirloom, we treat every watch with the care and respect it deserves.

Our Workshop

Our state-of-the-art workshop is equipped with the latest tools and technology to service even the most complex timepieces. Each watchmaker in our team undergoes rigorous training and continuous education to stay at the forefront of horological advancements.

We maintain a cleanroom environment for movement work, ensuring your watch is protected from dust and contaminants during service. Our precision timing machines and pressure testing equipment meet or exceed manufacturer specifications.

Every watch that enters our workshop receives individual attention from start to finish. We never rush the process, because true craftsmanship takes time.
