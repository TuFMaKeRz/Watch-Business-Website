Professional Watch Services

Comprehensive Watch Repair

Our certified watchmakers provide expert repair services for all types of watches, from vintage mechanical movements to modern smartwatches. We use only genuine parts and state-of-the-art equipment to ensure your timepiece receives the care it deserves.

Each repair begins with a thorough diagnosis to identify all issues. We then provide a detailed estimate before beginning any work, so you'll know exactly what to expect.

Precision Work

Our watchmakers have decades of combined experience working with luxury timepieces.

Genuine Parts

We source original manufacturer parts or equivalent quality replacements.

Warranty

All repairs come with a 2-year warranty on parts and labor.
